OBJECTIVE:
----------
The goal is to be the first player to run out of cards. The game ends immediately when a player plays their last card.

-----------------------------------
CORE RULES:
-----------------------------------
1. Setup:
   - A standard 40-card Spanish deck is used (Suits: Oros ðŸŸ¡, Copas ðŸŽ—ï¸, Espadas âš”ï¸, Bastos ðŸŒµ).
   - Each player is dealt 4 cards.
   - One card is placed face-up in the center to start the discard pile.

2. Turn Overview:
   - On your turn, you must play a card that matches the Rank (Number) OR the Suit (Color) of the top card on the discard pile.
   - Example: If the top card is "5 of Oros", you can play any "5" or any "Oros".
   - If you cannot play (or choose not to), you must DRAW one card from the deck.
   - If the drawn card is playable, you can play it immediately.

     The penalty then stacks to 4 cards for the player after them! (2 + 2 = 4, 4 + 2 = 6, etc.)
   - Strategy: Save these for defense or to attack the player next to you.
